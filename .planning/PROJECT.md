# My Portfolio 2026

## What This Is

A personal and professional software engineer portfolio that showcases selected web app projects and highlights design + engineering craft. It is designed for recruiters and tells a clear story of who I am with a minimal editorial aesthetic and confident, concise copy.

## Core Value

Recruiters can quickly understand who I am and see the quality of my design and engineering through a tight, curated project showcase.

## Requirements

### Validated

- ✓ Site serves a static homepage route in Astro — existing
- ✓ Tailwind CSS pipeline is available for global styling — existing

### Active

- [ ] Hero introduces name, role, and design + engineering focus in one clear statement
- [ ] Projects section showcases 3 web app projects with role, stack, and outcomes
- [ ] Each project card includes a concise summary and a primary link
- [ ] About section describes background, strengths, and working style in a short paragraph
- [ ] Services section lists offerings and the kind of work I take on
- [ ] Contact section includes primary CTA to email me
